Hello, My Name is aaron.

Aaron recently completed his HBA at Western University in Canada, focusing on international relations & French, with a particular interest in globalization & post-colonial politics. Years of passionate engagement in social issues have taken Aaron from the crèches of post-Apartheid townships in Cape Town to the bustling streets of Mumbai all the way to the hilltop villages of indígena in Eastern Colombia. Aaron’s avid interest in global affairs led to his selection as a Junior Team Canada ambassador on a trade and development mission to Central & South America, as well as a domestic mission to the Canadian Arctic. Upon returning from a year’s exchange in Europe & a development internship in Central Russia, Aaron co-founded the Canadian Youth Think Tank, an organization which offers opportunities for young Canadians to produce written articles on Canadian and global public policy news, developments & events. Aaron is also a World Vision Canada Youth Ambassador, a member of the Globe and Mail Student Advisory Board & a Junior Research Fellow at the NATO Council of Canada. Most recently, Aaron was elected as a City Youth Councillor in his hometown of Mississauga & he was also selected to be Canada’s Youth Ambassador to the Organization of Security and Co-operation in Europe, engaging in negotiations for a Youth & Security Action Plan. In October 2014, Aaron travelled to Ukraine as one of 90 Canadians to be an International Election Observer monitoring the Verkhovna Rada parliamentary elections. In the past, he has also met with Prince Charles & Desmond Tutu; climbed South Africa's iconic Lion's Head (by moonlight); & celebrated Christmas mass with the Pope. For his service, Aaron has most recently been recognized with the Queen's Young Leaders Award. For relaxation, Aaron enjoys listening to rock & a good read by the local harbour. In the long term, Aaron intends to pursue a career in international law & justice, & eventually in the Foreign Service.
